The problem
Great snow. Terrible traffic.

Every powder day, thousands of cars crawl up the canyons — most with empty seats. Meanwhile, riders scramble for rides, parking fills in minutes, and the canyons absorb the damage. There's a better way up.

How it works
1
Drivers post open seats
Set your resort, pickup time, and how many stops you'll make. You're in control of who you accept.
2
Riders browse and request
Find a seat by resort, time, and starting area. Post a ride request if nothing fits.
3
Drivers approve riders
No automatic matching. Drivers review and accept before anything is confirmed.
4
Rate each other after
Ratings build a trusted community over time — good drivers and riders rise to the top.

Cottonwood Carpool is built around community, not commerce. Drivers aren't paid. Riders don't pay fares. The only goal is getting more people up the canyon together.
